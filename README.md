## Task 3: Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis 🎯

### Objective
To analyze marketing funnel data to identify conversion drop-offs, channel performance, and opportunities to improve lead-to-customer conversion.

### Tools Used
* **Python** (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) - Data Extraction, Merging, and Funnel Analysis
* **Power BI** - Interactive Funnel & Performance Dashboard

### Key Business Insights
* **The Organic Advantage:** **Organic Search** is the highest performing acquisition channel, generating both the highest volume of leads (2,296) and the highest conversion rate (25.29%).
* **Paid Search Inefficiency:** Despite bringing in a high volume of traffic (1,586 leads), **Paid Search** struggles with a low conversion rate of 12.30%. This indicates a major drop-off between clicking the ad and completing the purchase.
* **Social Media Drop-off:** Social channels generate significant top-of-funnel interest but suffer the highest drop-off rate, converting at only 5.56%.

### Recommendations
* **Reallocate Ad Spend:** Shift 15-20% of the underperforming Paid Search and Social budgets toward SEO and Organic content creation to capitalize on the 25%+ conversion rate.
* **Optimize Paid Landing Pages:** Conduct targeted A/B testing on the landing pages linked to Paid Search ads to bridge the gap between click intent and final purchase.
